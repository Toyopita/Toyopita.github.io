Toyopita

Sound Engineer / Producer / Remixer

Artist Profile

Born in Osaka in 1992, Toyopita began his musical journey in 2019. A true all-rounder, he handles every aspect of music production from track-making to mastering. His unique sound fuses electronic, hip-hop, and R&B, transcending genre boundaries.

Career Highlights

  • 2021: Winner of the 1st and 2nd 8-bar Awards
  • 2021: Participated in Hokkaido rapper 4ceFINGER's EP "44 MAGNUM" under the name sudd
  • 2022: Remix of HIBIKILLA's "LUNA" featured on Apple Music's "Latest R&B Playlist"
  • 2022: Remix of HIBIKILLA's "Risin' to the Top feat. LAYA" featured on Spotify's official "Soul Music Japan" playlist
